Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about booking influencers through Eventero

Influencer rates vary based on their following size, engagement rate, and expertise. On Eventero, micro-influencers typically charge between ₦5,000 and ₦500,000 per event or promotion. Each influencer sets their own rates, which are clearly displayed on their profiles.
All payments are processed securely through Eventero. We accept multiple payment methods including credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and mobile money options. Funds are held in escrow until the influencer completes their agreed deliverables, ensuring protection for both parties.
In the rare case an influencer cancels or fails to deliver as agreed, Eventero provides a full refund. We also offer the option to quickly find a replacement influencer for your event, often with priority matching to minimize disruption.
