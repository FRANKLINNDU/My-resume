About This Event

Join us for the ultimate summer celebration at the Summer Music Festival 2025! Experience three days of incredible music across five stages featuring over 60 artists from around the world.

This year's lineup includes chart-topping headliners, indie favorites, and emerging artists spanning genres from pop and rock to electronic, hip-hop, and beyond. Beyond the music, enjoy gourmet food vendors, interactive art installations, wellness activities, and much more.

Whether you're coming for a single day or the full weekend experience, the Summer Music Festival offers unforgettable memories in one of New York's most iconic locations.

Festival Highlights:

  • 5 stages with continuous performances
  • 60+ artists across multiple genres
  • Gourmet food village with local vendors
  • Craft beer garden and specialty cocktails
  • Interactive art installations
  • Wellness area with yoga and meditation sessions
  • VIP experiences with exclusive viewing areas

Don't miss the biggest music event of the summer! Tickets are expected to sell out quickly, so secure yours today.

The Summer Music Festival takes place on the Great Lawn in Central Park, one of New York City's most iconic locations. The spacious venue offers a beautiful natural setting in the heart of Manhattan.

Getting There:

  • Subway: B or C train to 72nd Street, 6 train to 77th Street
  • Bus: M1, M2, M3, M4 to 79th Street
  • Parking: Limited street parking available. Public garages located on 83rd Street.

We encourage attendees to use public transportation as parking is limited in the area.

Frequently Asked Questions

What items can I bring to the festival?

You may bring small bags, blankets, sunscreen, empty reusable water bottles, and personal cameras. Large bags, outside food/drinks, professional cameras, and drones are prohibited.

Is the event rain or shine?

Yes, the festival will take place rain or shine. In case of severe weather, performances may be delayed or rescheduled.

Are tickets refundable?

All sales are final. Tickets cannot be refunded but may be transferred to another person through our official ticket exchange.

Is there an age restriction?

The festival is all ages. Children under 10 receive free admission when accompanied by a ticket-holding adult. VIP areas are 21+.

Can I leave and re-enter the festival?

Yes, re-entry is allowed with a valid wristband and same-day ticket.
