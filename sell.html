Common Questions

Find answers to frequently asked questions about selling tickets on TicketHub.

TicketHub charges a platform fee of 8-15% of the ticket price, depending on your event type and volume. There's also a standard payment processing fee of 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction. There are no setup fees, monthly fees, or hidden charges. You only pay when you sell tickets.

For most events, payouts are processed within 2-3 business days after ticket purchase. For larger events, you can choose between receiving payments as tickets are sold or getting a lump sum after your event. We also offer an advance funding option for eligible organizers.

Our micro-influencer marketplace connects you with content creators who can promote your event to their followers. You can browse influencers by category, location, and audience size, then book them directly through our platform. You only pay when an influencer accepts your booking, and you can track the performance of their promotion in your dashboard.

Yes! TicketHub offers extensive customization options for your event page. You can add your logo, choose your colors, upload custom images, and even use your own domain name. Our drag-and-drop editor makes it easy to create a professional-looking event page that matches your brand, no design skills required.

We offer 24/7 email support for all organizers, with priority phone support for events with over 100 tickets. Our help center has comprehensive guides and tutorials, and we provide personalized onboarding assistance for new organizers. We also offer dedicated account managers for larger events.

No, there are no contracts or long-term commitments. You can use TicketHub for a single event or for all your events. There are no penalties for not using the platform after creating an account. We believe in earning your business with every event.
